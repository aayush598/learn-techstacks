## HOW TO USE THIS BOOK

1. **Start with Part 1** — Build your foundation before anything else. Mindset, skills, and digital presence are prerequisites.
2. **Prioritize by relevance** — Jump to parts most aligned with your current goals, but don't skip Part 14 (Implementation Plan).
3. **Use Part 14 as your roadmap** — The 30/90/365-day plans tie everything together into a concrete action plan.
4. **Part 15 is your reference** — When you need to connect with someone specific, go directly to the relevant chapter.
5. **Templates in Part 14 Chapter 5** — Use the message templates for outreach. Don't send anything until you've customized it.

## YOUR VALUE PROPOSITION

Your primary asset in every interaction: **AI Agent Expertise**. Every chapter weaves this in — use it as your entry point to every domain.
