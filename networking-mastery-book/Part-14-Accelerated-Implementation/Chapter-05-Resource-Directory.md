### Section 4: Template: LinkedIn Connection Request Messages

#### Template A: General Professional

```
Hi [Name], I came across your profile while researching [topic/industry]. 
Your work at [Company] on [specific area] caught my attention.
I'm a technology graduate and a leading IT company entry-level hire exploring [your interest].
Would be great to connect and learn from your journey.
```

```
Hi [Name], your article/post about [specific topic] was really insightful. 
I especially liked your point about [specific detail].
I'm building [your project] in this space and appreciate your perspective.
Would love to connect!
```

#### Template C: Alumni Connection

Would be great to connect with a fellow [College] alum.

### Section 10: Conference/Event Networking Scripts

#### Before Event — Research Questions

For each speaker you want to meet, prepare:
1. What I like about their work (specific)
2. A question based on their recent work
3. What I can offer them (connection, tool, insight)

#### At Event — Opener (Person Standing Alone)

```
"Hi, great talk/event! I'm [Name]. What brought you here today?"

[Let them answer]

"That's interesting! I'm working on [your work]. How does [topic] connect with what you do?"
```

#### At Event — Opener (During Break)

```
"This is my first time at this event. Is it always this [adjective]?"

[Let them respond]

"I'm [Name], nice to meet you. I work on [your work]. What about you?"
```

#### At Event — Opener (Food/Drink Line)

```
"Hard choice with all the options! What's good here?"

[Laugh together]

"I'm [Name], by the way. First time at this event for me. You?"
```

#### At Event — Mid-Conversation Transitions

**Transition 1 (deeper):**
"You mentioned [topic]. I've been working on something similar. How do you handle [specific challenge]?"

**Transition 2 (exchange contacts):**
"This has been really interesting. Do you have a card or should we connect on LinkedIn?"

**Transition 3 (exit gracefully):**
"It was great meeting you. I don't want to monopolize your time — there are a few more people I want to catch. But let's definitely stay in touch!"

#### Virtual Event Scripts

**In chat:**
"Hi everyone! I'm [Name], [Title]. I'm joining because [reason]. Excited to learn from this session."

**In breakout room:**
"Hi everyone! I'm [Name], a [role] working on [domain]. What brings you to this session?"

**DM to speaker after:**
"Great talk! I particularly liked [specific point]. Here's a question I didn't get to ask: [question]."

### Section 18: Emergency Contact Building Strategies

**When you need contacts FAST (for a job, opportunity, or crisis):**

**Strategy 1: Warm Chain**
1. Find 5 people in your current network who know people in your target
2. Ask each for an introduction
3. Chain: You → Friend → Target

**Strategy 2: Conference Blitz**
1. Find a conference in your target domain (even if it's tomorrow)
2. Register and attend
3. In one day, you can build 5-10 genuine connections
4. Follow up within 24 hours

**Strategy 3: LinkedIn Advanced Search**
