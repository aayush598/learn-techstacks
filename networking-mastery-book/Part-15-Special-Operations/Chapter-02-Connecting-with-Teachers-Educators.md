### Why Educators? The Network Multiplier

Teachers and educators are the ultimate force multipliers. One educator influences thousands of students over decades. Connect with one influential educationist, and you open doors to generations of future leaders.

#### Conference Strategy for Educators

**Before conference:**
- Identify 10 professors/educators attending
- Read their recent publications or posts
- Prepare questions about their work

**During conference:**
- Attend paper presentation sessions (academic networking)
- Visit EdTech exhibition stalls (industry networking)
- Ask questions during Q&A
- Exchange cards with 5+ people per session

**After conference:**
- Connect on LinkedIn with specific reference
- Share something you learned
- "Your presentation on [topic] was excellent. I'm applying your framework to [my work]."

### Education Policy Connections

#### NEP 2020 — Your Conversation Starter

The National Education Policy 2020 is the biggest education reform in India. It emphasizes:
- Technology integration
- Multidisciplinary learning
- Critical thinking over rote learning
- Vocational training
- Indian languages

**Your AI angle for NEP conversations:**

### Education Technology and AI Opportunities

#### AI Applications in Education

**AI products you can build for education:**

1. **Personalized Learning Agent**: AI that adapts content to each student's pace

3. **Plagiarism Detection**: Advanced AI for Indian languages
4. **Career Guidance AI**: Suggest careers based on student profile
5. **College Recommendation System**: AI that predicts best fit colleges

### Teaching as a Networking Platform

#### Guest Lectures

**How to get guest lecture invitations:**
1. Build expertise in AI agents
2. Create a compelling talk title: "The Future of AI in Engineering"
3. Reach out to college professors:
   "Dear Professor, I'm a technology graduate working at a tech company, building AI agents. I'd love to deliver a free guest lecture to your students about AI in industry. Would you be open to this?"
4. After the lecture, the professor becomes a connection

**Guest lecture strategy for networking:**
- Target 1 college per month
- Connect with the professor and HOD
- Connect with interested students (they'll become professionals soon)
- Record the lecture and put it on YouTube
- Each lecture builds your credibility for the next

#### Workshops and Training

**Conduct paid workshops:**
- "AI Agents Workshop" for engineering students
- "Networking for Introverts" for college students
- "Career in AI" for final year students

**Pricing:**
- Free (at first, for reputation)
- ₹5000-15000 per workshop (1-2 days)
- Can be online or offline

**Each workshop = 20-50 new connections + testimonials**

### Final Words

Education is the most noble entry point for networking. Every educator is in the business of helping people grow. When you offer to help them help others, you speak their language.

Your university background is an ASSET in education — not a liability. You understand the struggles of students who don't go to IIT. Use that perspective. Build tools for THOSE students. Educators at all levels respect people who give back.

And remember: every professor, principal, and EdTech founder knows hundreds of other influential people. Invest in these relationships, and the returns compound across decades.
