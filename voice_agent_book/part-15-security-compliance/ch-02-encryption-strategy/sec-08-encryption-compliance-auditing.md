# Section 08: Encryption Compliance & Auditing

Encryption compliance ensures that encryption practices meet regulatory requirements (GDPR Article 32, HIPAA Security Rule, SOC 2 CC6.1). The compliance framework documents encryption standards, validates their implementation, and generates audit evidence. Regular audits verify that encryption controls are operating effectively.

Compliance controls: data classification matrix (which data requires encryption at rest, in transit, or both), encryption key management standards (NIST SP 800-57 key management guidelines), certificate management (automated renewal, revocation, and validation), and regular penetration testing targeting encryption implementations.

Audit evidence includes: encryption configuration snapshots, key rotation logs, certificate expiry reports, encryption algorithm version tracking, and third-party audit reports. The compliance dashboard shows real-time encryption status across all services, highlighting any non-compliant configurations for immediate remediation.
