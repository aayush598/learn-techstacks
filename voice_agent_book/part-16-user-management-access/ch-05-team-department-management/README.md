## Team Hierarchy

```
Tenant
 └── Sales Department
      ├── Outbound Sales Team
      │    ├── Senior Agents
      │    └── Junior Agents
      └── Inside Sales Team
           ├── Lead Qualification
           └── Closing Specialists
 └── Support Department
      ├── Tier 1 Support
      ├── Tier 2 Support
      └── Customer Success
 └── Operations Department
      └── Campaign Managers
```

- Design team hierarchy model with nested organizational structure
- Build department tree with multi-level reporting
- Implement team-level resource management and quotas

- Implement departmental billing allocation and chargeback
- Build team analytics and performance dashboards
- Handle team restructuring with data ownership migration
