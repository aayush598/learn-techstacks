# Section 07: Reseller Onboarding Flow

Reseller onboarding is a guided process that takes a new reseller from signup to active sub-account management. The onboarding flow educates resellers about the platform, configures their reseller profile, sets up their white-label configuration, creates their first sub-account, and provides training materials.

The onboarding steps: reseller agreement signing (digital contract), profile setup (company info, tax ID, bank account for payouts), tier assignment (automatic based on qualifications or manual), white-label configuration (domain, logo, colors), API key generation, training and certification (documentation, video courses, quiz), first sub-account creation (guided walkthrough), and go-live checklist. Each step has status tracking and can be revisited.

For a voice agent platform, reseller onboarding also includes configuring their reseller-specific settings: default sub-account tier and pricing, commission preferences, notification preferences, and support contact information. The entire onboarding should be completable in under 30 minutes.
