# Section 01: SLA Tier Definitions

SLA tiers define the service level commitments for each tenant plan. Tiers specify uptime guarantees, response times, resolution times, support availability, and credits for breaches. The SLA is a contractual commitment that drives operational processes and infrastructure investment.

Tier definitions include: availability (99.9% standard, 99.95% business, 99.99% enterprise), support response (4 hours standard, 1 hour business, 15 minutes enterprise), resolution time (24 hours standard, 8 hours business, 4 hours enterprise), support availability (business hours, 24/7 chat, 24/7 phone + dedicated engineer), and breach credits (5% monthly credit per 0.1% below SLA).

SLA configuration is stored per tenant and referenced by the monitoring system. Enterprise tenants can negotiate custom SLAs with specific metrics and credits. The SLA dashboard shows real-time compliance status against each commitment.
