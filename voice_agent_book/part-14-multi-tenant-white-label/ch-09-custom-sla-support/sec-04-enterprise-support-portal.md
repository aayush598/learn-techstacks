# Section 04: Enterprise Support Portal

The enterprise support portal is a dedicated interface for high-touch tenants with premium SLAs. It provides a white-label support experience with priority access: direct chat to senior engineers, ticket priority queuing, screen sharing, and access to the platform's internal knowledge base. The portal is accessible from the tenant's branded dashboard.

Portal features: 24/7 live chat with dedicated support team, ticket creation with priority SLA badges, escalation requests (one-click escalate to engineering management), scheduled maintenance calendar, known issues feed, feature request portal, and support analytics (response times, resolution times, satisfaction scores).

The enterprise portal also provides access to: tenant-specific runbooks (custom procedures agreed in contract), dedicated Slack/Discord channel, quarterly business reviews (scheduled meetings with account manager), and early access to new features. The portal is fully white-labeled with the tenant's branding.
