## Key Takeaways

- Multi-layered revenue: subscription + usage + marketplace + services
- Entry price point of $49/mo makes it accessible to SMBs
- Enterprise plans ($5000+/mo) drive majority of revenue
- Target gross margin: 70%+ at scale
- Payback period target: <6 months
