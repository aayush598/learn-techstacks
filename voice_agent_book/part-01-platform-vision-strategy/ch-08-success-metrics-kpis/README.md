## Key Takeaways

- North Star: Active agent minutes processed — directly correlates with revenue
- Activation: Time to first call under 5 minutes from signup
- Revenue target: NRR >120%, ARPU growth of 20% YoY
- Quality: Transcription accuracy >95%, call success >99%, uptime 99.9%
- Engineering: Deployment frequency daily, MTTR <1 hour, change failure rate <5%
