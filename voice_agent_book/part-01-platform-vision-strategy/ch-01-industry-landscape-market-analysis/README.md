## Key Takeaways

- Voice AI market is growing at 23.6% CAGR, reaching $31B+ by 2026
- Healthcare, finance, and retail are the top three verticals for voice AI adoption
- Open-source tools have matured enough to build production-grade systems
- Regulatory compliance is a competitive moat, not just a cost
- Real-time emotion and sentiment detection is the next major battleground
