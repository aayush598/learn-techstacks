# First 30 Connections Milestone

## Chapter 15: From Zero to Network

### The Milestone: Your First 30

Your first 30 connections are the hardest and most important. They form the foundation of everything that follows. These 30 people will:
- Be your initial node cluster
- Provide your first introductions
- Validate your existence to others
- Give you practice and confidence

### The 30-Person Target List

Create a target list of 30 specific people you WILL connect with in 90 days.

**Breakdown by type:**

| Type | Count | Examples |
|------|-------|----------|
| AI/ML peers | 8 | Other AI agent builders |
| Tech professionals | 5 | Software engineers, architects |
| Domain targets | 5 | Someone in finance, healthcare, etc. |
| Community leaders | 4 | Meetup organizers, influencers |
| Aspirational | 3 | Senior people in target domains |
| Local connectors | 3 | People who seem to know everyone |
| Mentors | 2 | People ahead of you in AI |

**Selection criteria for each:**
- Are they active? (posting, engaging, attending)
- Do they have connections I want? (connector potential)
- Can I add value to them? (AI agent expertise)
- Are they in my target domains?
- Would they plausibly respond?

### The Weekly Connection Sprint

Each week for 12 weeks, your goal is 2-3 new meaningful connections.

**Weekly Structure:**

```
Monday: Identify 5 new targets for the week
Tuesday: Engage with their content (comment, share)
Wednesday: Send connection request (personalized)
Thursday: If accepted, send DM with value
Friday: If no reply, send one follow-up
Weekend: Review, log, plan next week
```

### The First 10 (Weeks 1-4)

**Target: AI/ML Peers and Tech Professionals**

These are your easiest connections. You share domain, interests, and language.

**Strategy:**
1. Join 3 AI/ML Discord servers
2. Answer 10 questions thoroughly
3. DM 10 people with helpful resources
4. Connect on LinkedIn with everyone you helped

**Daily Actions (Weeks 1-4):**
- Spend 30 min in AI communities
- Answer 1-2 questions
- DM 2 people with value
- Send 3 LinkedIn connection requests
- Follow up on accepted requests

**Week 1 Targets:**
- 3 AI agent builders on LinkedIn
- 3 community members you helped
- 2 tech peers from online communities
- 2 local meetup attendees (if possible)

**Week 2 Targets:**
- Repeat with new people
- Re-engage previous connections
- Ask 1 for a 15-min call

**Week 3 Targets:**
- Expand to different communities
- Connect with people you haven't directly interacted with yet

**Week 4 Targets:**
- Your first 10 connections should be solid
- Schedule 2-3 calls with new connections

**Milestone Check: 10 connections + 3 ongoing conversations**

### The Second 10 (Weeks 5-8)

**Target: Domain Targets and Community Leaders**

These require more effort. You need to demonstrate value beyond your core domain.

**Strategy:**
1. Identify 10 people in target domains (finance, healthcare, etc.)
2. Study their work (read their content, understand their world)
3. Find the intersection with AI agents
4. Reach out with specific, valuable insights about the intersection

**Example outreach to a finance person:**
```
"I read your analysis on [topic]. Given my work in AI agents, I see an interesting application: [specific insight]. Thought you might find it valuable."
```

**Example outreach to a healthcare professional:**
```
"Your work on [topic] is fascinating. I've been building AI agents that could potentially [specific application]. Would love to get your professional perspective."
```

**Daily Actions (Weeks 5-8):**
- 30 min learning about 1-2 target domains
- 15 min engaging with domain-specific content
- Send 2-3 targeted outreach messages
- Provide value (resource, insight, introduction)

**Week 5-8 Targets:**
- 3 connections in healthcare/health tech
- 3 connections in finance/fintech
- 2 connections in government/civic tech
- 2 community organizers/influencers

**Milestone Check: 20 connections + 5+ cross-domain relationships**

### The Third 10 (Weeks 9-12)

**Target: Aspirational Connections and Mentors**

These are the hardest. They're busier, more selective, and have less reason to connect.

**Strategy:**
1. Leverage your existing 20 connections for warm intros
2. Build remarkable value (significant content, impressive project)
3. Use indirect approaches (comment on their content for weeks first)
4. Reach out with extreme specificity (show you've done your homework)

**Warm Introduction Template:**

Ask your existing connection:
```
"I've been following [Target's] work and I think there could be interesting synergy with what I'm building. Would you feel comfortable making an introduction? I'm happy to provide context."
```

**Cold Outreach for Aspirational Targets:**
```
Subject: [Specific reference to their work]

Dear [Name],

I've been following your work on [specific project/topic]. I was particularly struck by [specific detail].

I've been building [your project] and I believe there's an interesting connection between your work and mine. Specifically, [connection].

I know you're extremely busy. Would you be open to a 15-minute call next month? If not, no worries — I'll continue following your work.

Best,
[Your Name]
```

**Week 9-12 Targets:**
- 2 senior AI researchers or industry leaders
- 1 startup founder or CEO
- 1 person in government/policy
- 1 potential mentor
- 5 other high-value connections

**Milestone Check: 30 connections + 2 mentor relationships + 1 warm introduction chain started**
