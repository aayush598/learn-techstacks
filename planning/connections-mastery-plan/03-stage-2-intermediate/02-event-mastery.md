# Event Mastery: The Conference and Summit Strategy

## Chapter 18: Maximizing ROI from Every Event

### The Event Hierarchy

| Level | Type | Connection Potential | Cost | Frequency |
|-------|------|---------------------|------|-----------|
| 1 | Local meetups | 5-10 | Free-$10 | Weekly |
| 2 | Industry conferences | 10-50 | $50-$500 | Monthly |
| 3 | Major conferences | 20-100 | $100-$2,000 | Quarterly |
| 4 | Invite-only events | 50-200 | Free (by invite) | Rare |
| 5 | VIP/exclusive gatherings | 100+ | Very high | Rare |

### Before the Conference (2 weeks prior)

**Research:**
- Get attendee list (email organizers, check event app)
- Identify TOP 20 people you must meet
- Research each: recent work, interests, mutual connections
- Map their speaking schedule (when and where they'll be)

**Prepare:**
- Schedule meetings in advance (DM or email): "Will you be at [event]? Would love to grab coffee."
- Print business cards (or have digital ready)
- Prepare 3 conversation topics
- Practice your 30-second pitch

### During the Conference

**The First Hour (sets the tone):**
- Arrive early (before crowds)
- Find the registration desk (befriend organizers)
- Sit in front row (speakers notice you)
- Join attendee chat/app

**Session Strategy:**
- Don't attend every session (optimize for networking)
- Choose sessions aligned with your targets
- Sit next to people, not in empty rows
- Ask 1 question per session (people remember question-askers)

**Networking Session Strategy:**
- Work the room systematically (start at door, go clockwise)
- 5-7 minutes per conversation
- Exchange cards/contact info
- Note something distinctive about each person
- Move on: "Great meeting you. I want to make sure I connect with a few more people."

**Evening Events (where real connections happen):**
- Always attend evening socials
- Stay until near end (deeper conversations happen late)
- Don't drink too much
- Focus on quality, not quantity

### After the Conference

**Same Day:**
- Connect on LinkedIn with every person you met
- Include personalized message referencing your conversation
- Send 3-5 high-priority follow-ups immediately

**Within 48 Hours:**
- Send all follow-up messages and resources promised
- Schedule calls with top 10 connections
- Log everything in CRM

**Within 1 Week:**
- Send handwritten thank-you note to 3-5 key people
- Make introductions between new contacts
- Review and act on insights learned

### The VIP Strategy

**Getting Invited to Exclusive Events:**

1. Become a speaker (conferences invite speakers)
2. Become a sponsor's guest (connect with sponsors)
3. Become an organizer (volunteer)
4. Get introduced by someone already attending
5. Offer value to the organizer (media coverage, expertise)

**Action Items for Next Event:**
1. Research and register for 1 major event in next 90 days
2. Create pre-event target list (20 people)
3. Schedule 5 pre-event meetings with attendees
4. Prepare conversation topics and questions
5. Set post-event follow-up system
