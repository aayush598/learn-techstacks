# 100 Connections Milestone

## Chapter 20: Critical Mass Achieved

### The 100-Connection Network

With 100 connections, you have critical mass. Your network starts generating its own momentum:
- People mention you to others
- You appear in "People You May Know"
- Your content gets shared beyond your direct network
- You start receiving inbound connection requests

### The 100-Connection Breakdown

**Target Composition:**
- 30 AI/Tech peers (core domain)
- 20 Healthcare connections
- 20 Finance/Business connections
- 10 Government/Policy connections
- 10 Academic/Research connections
- 5 Legal connections
- 5 Military/Defense connections

**Relationship Level Distribution:**
- 10 Strategic/Strong (close relationships)
- 30 Active (regular interaction)
- 60 Casual/Surface (occasional contact)

### Consolidation Phase (Month 1-2 after 100)

**Don't add new connections for 60 days. Focus on deepening.**

**System:**
- Contact 10 connections per week (cycle through all 100 every 10 weeks)
- Each contact: add value, check in, share something
- Log every interaction in CRM

**Graduation Criteria for Stage 3:**
- [ ] 100+ connections across 6+ domains
- [ ] 10+ active cross-domain conversations
- [ ] 3+ strategic introductions made

- [ ] Known as a connector in your communities
- [ ] 3+ mentors or senior advocates
