# Daily Commandments

## The Connector's Daily Code

### The 10 Commandments

**I. I WILL ADD VALUE BEFORE I ASK FOR ANYTHING.**
Every interaction begins with giving. A resource, an insight, an introduction, a compliment. The giver sets the tone. I am the giver.

**II. I WILL REACH OUT TO AT LEAST ONE NEW PERSON.**
My network either grows or dies. Stagnation is decay. Every day, I extend one new branch.

**III. I WILL FOLLOW UP WITH SOMEONE I'VE ALREADY MET.**
The gold is in the follow-up. Relationships are built in the second, third, and tenth interaction — not the first.

**IV. I WILL LEARN SOMETHING ABOUT A PERSON OR DOMAIN I DON'T UNDERSTAND.**
Curiosity is my superpower. Every person knows something I don't. Every domain has something to teach me.

**V. I WILL LOG ONE INTERACTION IN MY SYSTEM.**
Memory is unreliable. Systems are trustworthy. I record what I learn, who I meet, and what I promise.

**VI. I WILL LISTEN MORE THAN I SPEAK.**
I have two ears and one mouth. Questions are more powerful than statements. I seek first to understand.

**VII. I WILL BE FULLY PRESENT IN EVERY CONVERSATION.**
The phone is away. The mind is focused. The person in front of me is the most important person in the world right now.

**VIII. I WILL REJECT REJECTION.**
A "no" is not about me. It's timing, context, or fit. I collect rejections like badges of honor — each one means I tried.

**IX. I WILL BE GRATEFUL FOR EVERY CONNECTION.**
Even the briefest interaction is a gift. Everyone who responds, meets, or helps me is doing so from their own precious time.

**X. I WILL REMEMBER THAT I STARTED FROM ZERO, AND TOMORROW I'LL BE FURTHER AHEAD.**
Today's efforts compound. The person who sends one message today is further than the person who sends none. Progress, not perfection.

### Morning Affirmation

Read this every morning:

```
Today I will:
- Add value first
- Reach out to someone new
- Follow up with someone I know
- Learn something I didn't know yesterday
- Log what I learn
- Listen more than I speak
- Be present in every conversation
- Not take rejection personally
- Be grateful for every interaction
- Remember that today I grow my network

I started from zero. Every day I build. 
My network is my net worth.
My value is my currency.
My consistency is my superpower.
```

### Evening Reflection

Answer these every evening:

```
1. Who did I add value to today? ___
2. Who did I reach out to? ___
3. Who did I follow up with? ___
4. What did I learn? ___
5. What will I do better tomorrow? ___
```

### The Commandments in Crisis

When you're tired, overwhelmed, or discouraged:

**Minimum viable day:**
1. Send one message (new or follow-up)
2. Learn one new thing
3. Log one interaction

That's it. Three actions. Even on your worst day, you can do this.

**Remember:** The network you build on good days sustains you on bad days. But you must build every day.
