# Regular Touchpoints

## The Maintenance Cadence System

### The Touchpoint Calendar

Create a systematic rotation for touching your connections:

```
Weekly: Strategic allies (top 10%)
Bi-weekly: Strong connections (next 20%)
Monthly: Active connections (next 30%)
Quarterly: Casual connections (remaining 40%)
```

### The Weekly Rotation System

With 100 connections:
- Week 1: Touch connections 1-25
- Week 2: Touch connections 26-50
- Week 3: Touch connections 51-75
- Week 4: Touch connections 76-100

Each connection gets touched monthly. Takes ~5 minutes per connection = ~2 hours/month total.

### Touchpoint Types by Relationship Stage

| Stage | Touch Type | Example |
|-------|-----------|---------|
| New connection (<1 month) | Follow-up | Share resource, suggest call |
| Early (1-3 months) | Engagement | Comment on their content, DM |
| Growing (3-12 months) | Value delivery | Make intro, offer help |
| Established (1+ year) | Deeper connection | Personal check-in, collaboration |
| Strategic alliance (2+ years) | Mutual support | Regular calls, joint projects |

### The Touchpoint Content

**What to say in a maintenance touch:**

**Option 1: Resource share (low effort)**
```
"Came across this and thought of you — [link]. Hope you're doing well!"
```

**Option 2: Check-in (medium effort)**
```
"Hey [Name], been thinking about our conversation on [topic]. How's [project/challenge] going?"
```

**Option 3: Question (medium effort)**
```
"I'm working on [something relevant to them] and would love your perspective. What's your take on [specific question]?"
```

**Option 4: Value offer (higher effort)**
```
"I recently connected with someone working on [relevant area] — would you like an introduction?"
```

### The Seasonal Cadence

**Quarterly themes for systematic contact:**

| Quarter | Theme | Action |
|---------|-------|--------|
| Q1 (Jan-Mar) | New Year Reset | Check goals, offer support |
| Q2 (Apr-Jun) | Mid-Year Pulse | How's their year going? |
| Q3 (Jul-Sep) | Fall Planning | What's next for them? |
| Q4 (Oct-Dec) | Year-End Gratitude | Thank them, celebrate wins |

### The Birthday System

LinkedIn shows birthdays. Use this:

**Template:**
```
"Happy birthday, [Name]! Hope you have a fantastic day. Wishing you a great year ahead."
```

**For closer connections (add personal touch):**
```
"Happy birthday, [Name]! Hope you're celebrating well. Thinking about our conversation on [topic] earlier this year — hope things are going great with that."
```

### Weekly Touchpoint Batch

**Every Friday (30 min):**
1. Check CRM for connections due for contact
2. Go through each: send message (5 min each)
3. Log the contact
4. Schedule next touchpoint

### Avoiding the "Random Contact" Trap

Don't contact people randomly saying "just checking in" with no substance. Always include:
- A specific reference to them
- Something of value
- A reason for reaching out

**Bad:** "Hey, just checking in. How are you?"
**Good:** "Hey [Name], I was reading about [their industry] and thought of your point about [specific]. Also came across this resource that might interest you: [link]. Hope you're doing well!"

### Automated Reminders

Set up your CRM to remind you:

**Daily:**
- People whose follow-up date is today

**Weekly:**
- People who haven't been contacted in 30+ days

**Monthly:**
- People who haven't been contacted in 90+ days (dormant alert)

### The 80/20 Rule of Maintenance

20% of your connections will provide 80% of your value. But you don't know which 20% yet.

Maintain ALL connections. The most valuable connection might be the one you least expect.
