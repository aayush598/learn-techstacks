# Network Health Audit

## Assessing and Improving Your Network's Vitality

### Why Audit Your Network

Networks decay without attention. An annual audit reveals:
- Which relationships are strong and which are dying
- Where your network has gaps
- Which domains you're neglecting
- Where you should focus your energy

### The Network Improvement Plan

Based on the audit, create a 90-day plan:

| Priority | Action | Timeline | Success Metric |
|----------|--------|----------|----------------|
| 1 | Re-engage 20 dormant connections | 30 days | 10 positive responses |
| 2 | Enter healthcare domain | 60 days | 10 new healthcare connections |
| 3 | Deepen 5 strategic alliances | 90 days | Regular contact established |
| 4 | ... | ... | ... |

### The 90-Day Follow-Up

After 90 days, review the improvement plan:
- Did you execute the priority actions?
- Did the metrics improve?
- What new issues emerged?
- What worked and what didn't?

### Keeping the Audit Honest

**Hard questions to ask yourself:**
- Am I collecting connections or building relationships?
- Am I giving more than I take?
- Am I connecting with people who matter or people who are easy?
- Is my network diversified or am I in an echo chamber?
- Would my network show up for me if I needed them?
