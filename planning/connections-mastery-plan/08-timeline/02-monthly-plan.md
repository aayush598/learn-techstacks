# Monthly Plan

## 30-Day Sprints

### Monthly Theme Rotation

| Month | Theme | Focus |
|-------|-------|-------|
| Month 1 | Foundation | Profile setup, systems, knowledge base |
| Month 2 | Volume | High-volume outreach, first 50 connections |
| Month 3 | Depth | Deepen first 50, add 25 more |
| Month 4 | Content | Launch content engine, build audience |
| Month 5 | Events | Attend 2+ events, do 10+ in-person meetings |
| Month 6 | Cross-Domain | Enter 1 new domain, get 10 connections there |
| Month 7-8 | Strategic Alliances | Convert 10 connections to alliances |
| Month 9-10 | Thought Leadership | Speaking, guest posting, media |
| Month 11-12 | Ecosystem | Launch community, host events |

### Monthly Targets

**Phase 1 (Months 1-6):**
- New connections: 30-50
- Calls completed: 10-15
- Events attended: 3-5
- Content published: 12-20 pieces
- Cross-domain connections: 10+

**Phase 2 (Months 7-12):**
- New connections: 50-100
- Strategic alliances: 3-5
- Speaking engagements: 1-2
- Guest posts/articles: 2-3
- New domains entered: 2

**Phase 3 (Year 2):**
- New connections: 100-200
- Major projects/collaborations: 2-3
- Conference speaking: 2-3
- Media appearances: 3-5
- Ecosystem growth: Launch community
