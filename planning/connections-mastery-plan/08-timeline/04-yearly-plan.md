# Yearly Plan

## Annual Milestones

### Year 1: Zero to 500

**Theme:** Emergence

**Targets:**
- Connections: 500 (all digital, some in-person)
- Domains covered: 10
- Content: 200+ pieces published
- Events: 25+ attended
- Speaking: 3+ events
- Mentors: 3+
- Revenue from network: Not expected

**Monthly averages:**
- 42 new connections/month
- 17 content pieces/month
- 2 events/month

**By year end you should have:**
- Active presence across LinkedIn, Twitter, GitHub
- Regular content creation habit
- 500+ connections across 10+ domains
- At least 20 people who would take your call
- 3+ ongoing mentorship relationships
- Clear reputation as AI agent expert + connector

### Year 2: 500 to 2,000

**Theme:** Acceleration

**Targets:**
- Connections: 2,000
- Domains covered: 15
- Speaking: 10+ events
- Alliances: 10+
- Media appearances: 5+
- First advisory role

**Key shifts:**
- More in-person than digital
- More inbound than outbound
- More quality than quantity
- More strategic than tactical

### Year 3: 2,000 to 5,000

**Theme:** Power

**Targets:**
- Connections: 5,000
- Global reach: 10+ countries
- Board/advisory positions: 3+
- Ecosystem built: Active community of 500+
- Revenue from network: Significant opportunities

### Year 4: 5,000 to 10,000

**Theme:** Mastery

**Targets:**
- Connections: 10,000
- Global network: 20+ countries
- Media: Regular columnist or quoted expert
- Speaking: Keynote invitations
- Legacy: Successors being developed

### Year 5: 10,000 to 20,000+

**Theme:** Legacy

**Targets:**
- Connections: 20,000+
- Institution built: Foundation, institute, or fund
- Successors independent and thriving
- Known as: One of the most connected people in AI globally
