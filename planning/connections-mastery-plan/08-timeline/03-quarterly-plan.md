# Quarterly Plan

## 90-Day Milestones

### Year 1: The Foundation

**Q1 (Months 1-3): Zero to First 50**
- Goal: 50 connections across 3+ domains
- Content: 30+ pieces published
- Skills: Communication, outreach, CRM systems
- Events: 3+ attended
- Key milestone: First warm introduction received

**Q2 (Months 4-6): Building Momentum**
- Goal: 150 connections across 5+ domains
- Content: 60+ pieces published
- Skills: Deepening, cross-domain bridging
- Events: 8+ attended
- Key milestone: First inbound connection from high-value person

**Q3 (Months 7-9): Strategic Shift**
- Goal: 300 connections across 7+ domains
- Content: 100+ pieces, first guest post
- Skills: Thought leadership, public speaking
- Events: 15+ attended, first speaking engagement
- Key milestone: Known as connector in 3 communities

**Q4 (Months 10-12): Consolidation**
- Goal: 500 connections across 10+ domains
- Content: Newsletter launched, 150+ pieces
- Skills: Alliance building, ecosystem thinking
- Events: 25+ attended, 3+ speaking
- Key milestone: First cross-domain project facilitated

### Year 2: Acceleration

**Q1-Q2:** 1,000+ connections, major speaking, ecosystem launch
**Q3-Q4:** 2,000+ connections, advisory roles, media presence

### Year 3: Power

**Full year:** 5,000+ connections, board positions, significant influence

### Year 4: Mastery

**Full year:** 10,000+ connections, global network, legacy building

### Year 5: Legacy

**Full year:** Institutionalized network, successors developed, global recognition
