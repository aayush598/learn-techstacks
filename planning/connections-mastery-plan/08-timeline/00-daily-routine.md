# Daily Routine: The Operating System

## Your Day-by-Day Blueprint

### The Core Daily (Phase 1-2)

| Time | Activity | Duration | Notes |
|------|----------|----------|-------|
| 6:00-6:30 | Wake + Morning routine | 30 min | Exercise (bodyweight), shower, grooming |
| 6:30-7:00 | Knowledge input | 30 min | News, 1 domain deep-dive, AI paper |
| 7:00-7:30 | Content creation | 30 min | Write 1 post, record 1 insight |
| 7:30-8:30 | Deep work (AI projects) | 60 min | Build portfolio, strengthen core skill |
| 8:30-9:00 | Morning outreach | 30 min | Send 5 connection requests, engage with 10 posts |
| 9:00-12:00 | Primary work/study | 180 min | Deep work on priority tasks |
| 12:00-13:00 | Lunch + networking | 60 min | 1 call OR 5 DMs OR publish content |
| 13:00-17:00 | Secondary work/study | 240 min | Continue projects, learning |
| 17:00-17:30 | Evening outreach | 30 min | Follow-ups, reply to messages, CRM update |
| 17:30-19:00 | Break/Exercise | 90 min | Walk, rest, exercise |
| 19:00-20:00 | Event/Community | 60 min | Attend online event OR community engagement |
| 20:00-21:00 | Reading/learning | 60 min | Book, long-form article, podcast |
| 21:00-21:30 | Review & Planning | 30 min | Log connections, review day, plan tomorrow |
| 21:30-22:00 | Wind down | 30 min | Prepare for sleep |
| 22:00-6:00 | Sleep | 8 hours | Non-negotiable |

### The Core Daily (Phase 3-5)

| Time | Activity | Notes |
|------|----------|-------|
| 6:00-7:00 | Morning routine + knowledge | Compressed |
| 7:00-8:30 | Strategic work | Focus on high-value outreach |
| 8:30-12:00 | Primary work | AI projects, client work |
| 12:00-14:00 | Networking lunch | 1-2 meetings |
| 14:00-17:00 | Secondary work | Content, operations |
| 17:00-19:00 | Evening events | Networking events |
| 19:00-20:00 | Calls with global connections | Time zone management |
| 20:00-21:00 | Strategic planning | Review, adjust |
| 21:00-22:00 | Wind down | Rest |

### Daily Non-Negotiables (Every Phase)

1. Add value to at least 1 person
2. Make at least 1 new connection attempt
3. Follow up on at least 1 existing connection
4. Learn something new about a target domain
5. Log all interactions in CRM

### Daily Checklist

```
MORNING:
[ ] Exercise (15 min minimum)
[ ] Read news/knowledge (15 min)
[ ] Write/publish content (1 piece)
[ ] Morning outreach (5 DMs, 10 engagements)

AFTERNOON:
[ ] 1 call or meeting (or schedule one)
[ ] Deep work on AI project (1 hour)
[ ] Follow up on messages

EVENING:
[ ] Attend event OR community engagement
[ ] CRM update
[ ] Plan next day
[ ] Review wins and lessons
```
