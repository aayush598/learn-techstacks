# Value Delivery Methods

## 50+ Ways to Add Value to Your Connections

### The Value Delivery Matrix

| Effort | Time | Impact | Method |
|--------|------|--------|--------|
| Low | <2 min | Low-Medium | Like, share, quick congratulation |
| Low | <5 min | Medium | Send article with note, comment thoughtfully |
| Medium | 10-30 min | Medium-High | Write recommendation, make small intro |
| Medium | 30-60 min | High | Review project, provide consultation |
| High | 1-3 hours | Very High | Strategic intro, write case study, collaborate |

### Low Effort Value (<5 min)

**Digital signals:**
1. Like their LinkedIn post (signals support)
2. Share their content with your network (amplification)
3. Comment thoughtfully on their post (adds to discussion)
4. Quote-tweet them on Twitter (endorsement)
5. Congratulate them on milestones (birthday, work anniversary, promotion)

**Quick shares:**
6. "Saw this and thought of you" — send relevant article with one line
7. Tag them in a relevant discussion
8. Send a relevant job posting
9. Recommend a tool they might find useful
10. Forward an event invitation

### Medium Effort Value (5-30 min)

**Digital contributions:**
11. Write them a LinkedIn recommendation
12. Endorse 3+ of their skills
13. Write a detailed comment on their article
14. Record a 2-minute voice note with your thoughts
15. Create a graphic highlighting their work

**Connections:**
16. Introduce them to one person (low-effort intro)
17. Invite them to a group/community
18. Suggest someone they should follow
19. Connect them with a potential collaborator
20. Recommend them for a speaking opportunity

**Knowledge:**
21. Send a book recommendation with why it fits them
22. Share a framework or template you use
23. Answer a question they posted publicly
24. Send a summary of a podcast they'd enjoy
25. Share your notes from a conference talk

### High Effort Value (30 min - 2 hours)

**Strategic:**
26. One-hour strategic consultation on AI agents
27. Review their project/business and give feedback
28. Write a detailed case study featuring their work
29. Record a testimonial video for their product
30. Spend a day thinking about their biggest challenge

**Connections:**
31. Organize a dinner/meetup and invite them
32. Make a high-stakes warm introduction
33. Connect them with a potential investor/client
34. Host an event and feature them as speaker
35. Circulate their resume/CV to your network

**Content:**
36. Write a guest post for their blog
37. Interview them for your podcast/newsletter
38. Feature them in your "people to follow" list
39. Co-author an article with them
40. Create a detailed resource compilation for them

### Ultra-High Value (2+ hours)

**Career-changing:**
41. Recommend them for a board position
42. Nominate them for an award
43. Advocate for them in a hiring decision
44. Fund their project or connect them with funding
45. Offer them a partnership or joint venture

**Life-changing:**
46. Connect them with a mentor who transforms their trajectory
47. Help them navigate a career crisis
48. Provide emotional support during difficult time
49. Defend them publicly when they're under attack
50. Invest your time, reputation, and resources in their success

| Tue | [Name] | Send tool recommendation | Low |
| Wed | [Name] | Write LinkedIn recommendation | Medium |
| Thu | [Name] | Introduce to potential collaborator | Medium |
| Fri | [Name] | Give feedback on their project | High |

### The Value Ratio

Track your giving vs. taking:

```
This week:
- Times I gave value: ___
- Times I asked for something: ___
- Ratio (target: 5:1 or higher): ___
```

If your ratio drops below 3:1, stop asking and start giving.
