# Note-Taking Systems

## Capturing and Organizing Connection Intelligence

### Why Notes Matter

Your memory will fail you. Notes make you:
- **Thoughtful** — You remember details others forget
- **Organized** — You track conversations over time
- **Valuable** — You can reference past discussions
- **Trustworthy** — You follow up on promises

### The Zettelkasten for Connections

Each connection is a "note" linked to other notes:
- People you know → Their domains → Their interests
- Conversations → Topics → Resources shared
- Introductions → People introduced → Outcomes

### The Daily Conversation Log

After every meaningful interaction, capture:

```
Date: [Date]
Person: [Name]
Context: [Where/how we interacted]

Key discussion points:
1. [Their current work/project]
2. [Challenges they're facing]
3. [Interests/opportunities they mentioned]

Value I provided:
- [What I gave them]

Value I received:
- [What they gave me]

Promises made:
- [I promised to...]
- [They promised to...]

Next steps:
- [Follow-up action, date]

Personal notes:
- [Family, interests, personality observations]
```

### The Interaction Timeline

For key connections, maintain a timeline:

```
[Name] — Relationship Timeline

Date 1 (Jan 2025): Met at [event]. Discussed [topic].
Date 2 (Feb 2025): Coffee meeting. Shared [resource]. Connected them with [person].
Date 3 (Apr 2025): Collaborated on [project]. 
Date 4 (Jul 2025): Helped them with [problem]. They introduced me to [person].
...
```

### The Domain Knowledge Notes

For each domain, maintain:

```
Domain: [Finance, Healthcare, etc.]

People I know in this domain:
- [Name] — Role — Relationship strength
- [Name] — Role — Relationship strength

Key concepts learned:
1. [Concept]
2. [Concept]

Current trends:
1. [Trend]
2. [Trend]

Conversation hooks (questions to ask people in this domain):
1. [Question]
2. [Question]

AI agent opportunities in this domain:
1. [Opportunity]
2. [Opportunity]
```

### The Promise Tracker

Track everything you promise:

```
Promise | Person | Date Promised | Deadline | Status
-------|--------|---------------|----------|-------
Send article about X | [Name] | Jan 5 | Jan 12 | Done
Intro to [Person] | [Name] | Jan 10 | Jan 20 | Pending
Review their project | [Name] | Jan 15 | Jan 25 | Not started
```

Check this list daily. Unfulfilled promises damage your reputation.

### Tool Recommendations

**Obsidian (Recommended):**
- Local-first, markdown-based
- Link notes to each other (graph view)
- Tags and folders for organization
- Templates for structured notes

**Notion:**
- Database features for structured data
- Good for CRM-style tracking
- Templates and galleries

**Google Docs:**
- Simple, accessible anywhere
- Create a folder per domain
- Use templates

### The Weekly Note Review

Every Sunday, review your notes:

```
1. New connections this week: [Review and log]
2. Promises made: [Any unfulfilled?]
3. Key insights gathered: [What did you learn?]
4. Follow-ups needed: [Who hasn't been responded to?]
5. Notes to clean up: [Any missing details?]
```
