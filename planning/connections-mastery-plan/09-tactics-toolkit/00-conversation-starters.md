# Tactics Toolkit

## Conversation Starters

**For any domain:**
1. "What's the biggest change you've seen in [field] in the last 5 years?"
2. "What's a common belief in [field] that you disagree with?"
3. "If you could wave a magic wand and change one thing about [field], what would it be?"
4. "What's the most interesting project you're working on right now?"
5. "What do you wish more people understood about [field]?"

**For tech people:**
- "What's your take on the current state of AI agents?"
- "What technical challenge keeps you up at night?"
- "Which technology from another field do you wish existed in yours?"

**For non-tech people:**
- "How do you see technology affecting your industry?"
- "What's the biggest misconception about your work?"
- "What's one thing in your field that you think deserves more attention?"

## Follow-Up Systems

**The 1-3-7-30 Follow-Up Sequence:**
- Day 1: Connect + thank you
- Day 3: Share relevant resource
- Day 7: Propose next step
- Day 30: Check in

**Dormant connection re-engagement (60+ days):**
- "I came across [something] and thought of you — hope you're doing well!"
- "It's been a while! How have you been? I'd love to catch up."

## Meeting Request Templates

**Email:**
```
Subject: [Topic] — [Your Name] / [Mutual Connection]

Hi [Name],

[Context — how you found them, why reaching out]
[Value — what you can offer or discuss]
[Ask — specific, low-friction request]

Would you be open to a 15-minute call [timeframe]?

Best,
[Your Name]
```

**LinkedIn:**
```
Hi [Name], I've been following your work on [topic]. I'm building [project] and I think there could be interesting synergy. Would you be open to a brief chat?
```

## Value Delivery Methods

**Low effort, high value:**
- "I saw this and thought of you" (article, tool, opportunity)
- Public shout-out on LinkedIn/twitter
- 5-minute intro to someone useful
- Quick feedback on something they're working on

**Medium effort, high value:**
- Written introduction email
- Coffee meeting (your treat)
- Review of their project/work
- Resource compilation

**High effort, high value:**
- Strategic consultation
- Warm introduction to VIP
- Collaboration on project
- Public advocacy
