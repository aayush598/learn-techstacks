# Measurement & Tracking

## Measuring What Matters

### The KPIs of Network Power

**Leading Indicators (predict future success):**
1. Outreach volume (messages sent per day)
2. Response rate (% of cold messages that get replies)
3. Connection depth (average relationship strength)
4. Domain diversity (number of domains with 10+ connections)
5. Value delivery (introductions made, resources shared)

**Lagging Indicators (measure past success):**
1. Total connection count
2. Inbound request rate
3. Opportunities generated (jobs, deals, collaborations)
4. Status/reach (content impressions, speaking invitations)
5. Network value (ability to reach anyone within X degrees)

### The Network Value Assessment

**Question 1: Access**
- Can I reach a minister/government official within 2 degrees? [Y/N]
- Can I reach a CEO of a major company within 2 degrees? [Y/N]
- Can I reach a top scientist/researcher within 2 degrees? [Y/N]

### The 6-Month Deep Dive

**Every 6 months, do a comprehensive audit:**

```
1. NETWORK CARTOGRAPHY
Map your top 100 connections by domain.
Identify gaps: which domains are underweight?
Identify bridges: who connects across domains?

2. RELATIONSHIP HEALTH
For top 50 connections, assess:
- When was last contact?
- What value have we exchanged?
- Is the relationship growing or stagnating?
- What's the next step?

3. STRATEGY REVIEW
- What worked in the last 6 months?
- What didn't work?
- What domains should I focus on next?
- What skills do I need to develop?

4. GOAL RESET
- Network size target for next 6 months: ___
- Domains to enter: ___
- Key people to connect with: ___
- Skills to develop: ___
- Content/output targets: ___
```

### The Ultimate Metric

**The single question that matters most:**

> "Can I make one phone call and accomplish something that would take someone else weeks or months?"

When the answer is "yes" for multiple domains, you have achieved network mastery.
