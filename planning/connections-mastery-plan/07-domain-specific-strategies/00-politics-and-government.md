# Domain-Specific Strategies

## Politics & Government

### Understanding the Political Landscape

**Key entry points:**
- Political party youth wings
- Policy think tanks
- University political science departments
- Election campaigns (volunteer)
- Constituent services (help with local issues)

### Building Political Connections

**The political networking ladder:**

1. **Local level** — Municipal councilors, local party officials
2. **State/Regional level** — MLAs, MLCs, state ministers
3. **National level** — MPs, ministers, party leadership
4. **Executive level** — Secretaries, advisors, bureaucrats

**Approach strategy:**
- Start with their advisors and staff (gatekeepers)
- Attend public meetings and town halls
- Offer expertise on AI/tech policy
- Be a resource, not a supplicant

### What Politicians Need

**Value you can provide:**
- Understanding of technology and AI (most don't)
- Connections to tech industry
- Data analysis and insights
- Campaign technology support
- Policy research on tech issues

**Outreach template:**
```
"Dear [Politician/Advisor],

I'm an AI agent expert and I've been thinking about [policy issue] and how technology could help. I'd love to share some ideas if you're open to a brief discussion.

I understand you're busy, so I'll be brief and specific.

Best,
[Your Name]"
```

### Government Bureaucracy

**Key people:** Secretaries, joint secretaries, directors, undersecretaries

**Approach:**
- Formal channels first (email, official meetings)
- Through industry associations
- Through conferences and events
- Through mutual connections

**The bureaucratic mindset:**
- They value process and protocol
- They need to protect against risk
- They respect expertise and credentials
- They appreciate those who understand their constraints

### Government Consulting Pathway

1. Build expertise in AI policy
2. Write white papers on specific issues
3. Get published in policy journals
4. Speak at government-organized events
5. Get invited to consultation committees
6. Become a formal advisor

### Key Political Figures to Target

Create a target list based on your country's political structure:
- Ministers of: Technology, Health, Finance, Education, Defense
- Opposition counterparts (non-partisan relationships)
- Senior bureaucrats in key ministries
- Policy advisors and think tank heads
