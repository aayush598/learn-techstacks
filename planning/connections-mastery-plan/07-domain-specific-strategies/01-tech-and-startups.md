# Tech & Startups

## Your Home Domain — Build from Strength

### The Tech Ecosystem Map

```
Investors (VCs, Angels)
    ↕
Startups (Founders, CTOs, Engineers)
    ↕
Big Tech (Google, Microsoft, Meta, Amazon)
    ↕
Research (Universities, Labs)
    ↕
Community (Meetups, Conferences, Online)
```

### Connecting with Tech Leaders

**CTOs and Engineering Leaders:**
- Engage with their technical content
- Share your own technical work
- Offer specific technical insights
- "I built something that solves [common problem] — would love your feedback"

**Founders and CEOs:**
- Understand their business challenges
- Show how AI agents apply to their industry
- Offer introductions to potential customers or partners
- Be brief, be valuable, be respectful of their time

**VCs and Investors:**
- Track their portfolio companies
- Provide insights on AI agent landscape
- Offer introductions to promising founders
- Attend demo days and startup events

### The Startup Community Strategy

**Week 1-2:** Attend startup meetups
**Week 3-4:** Start engaging with startup content online
**Month 2:** Offer free AI agent consultation to 3 startups
**Month 3:** Build relationships with 5 founders
**Month 4-6:** Get introduced to VCs through founders

### Your AI Agent Advantage in Tech

**Position yourself:**
- "The go-to person for AI agent architecture"
- "Someone who's built production agent systems"
- "The bridge between AI agents and specific industries"
