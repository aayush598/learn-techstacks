# Cross-Continental Bridges

## Chapter 32: The Global Connector

### The Bridge Builder Identity

At the master level, you're known for bridging continents. You connect:
- Silicon Valley with Singapore
- London with Dubai
- Berlin with Tokyo
- New York with Mumbai

### The Multi-Continent Strategy

**Choose 5 continental hubs:**

1. **North America:** San Francisco / New York
2. **Europe:** London / Berlin
3. **Asia:** Singapore / Tokyo / Bangalore
4. **Middle East:** Dubai / Tel Aviv
5. **Oceania:** Sydney

**For each hub, build:**
- 20+ strong connections
- Knowledge of the local ecosystem
- Regular visits (annual minimum)
- Local advocates who know you

### The Global Routine

**Annual travel cadence:**
- Quarter 1: Asia (4 weeks)
- Quarter 2: Europe (4 weeks)
- Quarter 3: North America (4 weeks)
- Quarter 4: Middle East + rest (4 weeks)

**In each location:**
- Week 1: Deep work on AI projects
- Week 2: Networking (20+ meetings)
- Week 3: Events and speaking
- Week 4: Exploration + weaker connections

### Virtual Global Presence

Between travels, maintain global presence:
- Schedule calls across time zones (rotate to be fair)
- Join international online communities
- Create content that resonates globally
- Stay current on global events in all target regions

### The 5000-Connection Milestone

With 5,000+ connections across 15+ domains and 20+ countries:
- You can get a message to almost anyone within 3 degrees
- People seek you out for your network
- You have influence across sectors and borders
- You're a known entity in multiple ecosystems
- Your network generates more opportunities than you can pursue
