# Legacy Building: Networks That Outlive You

## Chapter 29: The Institutional Network

### Beyond Personal Network

A personal network dies with you. An institutional network lives on.

**Institutionalize your network:**
1. **Create archives** — Document your relationships (anonymized for privacy)
2. **Build systems** — Not dependent on any single person (including you)
3. **Develop successors** — People who carry forward your connecting mission
4. **Found institutions** — Organizations, foundations, communities that outlast you

### Your Legacy Platform

Choose your legacy vehicle:
- **A foundation** (funds cross-domain AI projects)
- **An institute** (research and policy on AI agents)
- **A community** (global network of AI agent builders)
- **A fund** (invests in AI agent startups)
- **A media platform** (educates about AI agent opportunities)

### Developing Successors

Identify and mentor 5-10 people who will carry forward:
- Your network map
- Your connecting philosophy
- Your ecosystem platforms
- Your values and standards

### The Network Documentation Project

Create a comprehensive guide:
- Connection principles and philosophy
- Systems and tools used
- Key relationships and history
- Domain knowledge accumulated
- Succession plan
