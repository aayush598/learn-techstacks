**Q4. Describe a challenging situation during your internship and how you resolved it.**
**Answer:** During my Agentic AI Internship at Krip AI, a major challenge was minimizing the latency of our LLM API calls while integrating them into our FastAPI backend. The initial implementation was synchronous, causing timeouts for long queries. I resolved this by transitioning the endpoints to handle asynchronous execution using Python's `asyncio` and separating the heavy AI inferences into background tasks. This massively improved the throughput and user experience of our application.

**Q6. Tell us about a time you worked in a team under pressure.**
